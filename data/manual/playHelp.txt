CONTROLS

The main keys for playing the game are: $leftKey$rightKey$upKey$downKey, $jumpKey and $contextKey.

You can exit the level anytime by hitting ESC.

You can switch from and to fullscreen mode with either F11 or Alt+Enter.


Nikki's Controls:
$leftKey$rightKey - move left and right
$jumpKey - jump
$contextKey - use a terminal

Terminal Menu Controls:
$leftKey$rightKey - select a robot (or eject)
$jumpKey - confirm your choice

Robot Controls:
$leftKey$rightKey$upKey$downKey - depends on the robot
$jumpKey - depends on the robot
$contextKey - go back to the terminal menu
